ARTIFACTS EXHIBITION

ANNOTATED BIBLIOGRAPHY

2023

The annotated bibliography was a writing product that summarized the sources that were related to my research. While gathering secondary sources for the annotated bibliography, I had to determine whether or not it was relevant to my research or genre. After I thought it was good, I put it into my annotated bibliography and started "dissecting" the sources. I looked for the methods, the results, etc. With this assignment I was able to learn what "good" sources were and determine what would be beneficial for my research which would then be presented to the community I was aiming at.

LITERATURE REVIEW

2023

The literature review was the first assignment where I put together a writing product by looking at multiple of sources related to my research and chosen genre. The annotated bibliography helped me organize my thoughts while the literature review put the sources together and helped me figure out my gap for the study. In relation to the outcome, I was able to figure out what sources were important and relevant to my study and to the community I was aiming at.
