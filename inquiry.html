FREEWRITE

2023

Freewrites were done during class and had questions that jogged our minds to think about our research and help us process and think what we were going to be doing in our research. Through freewrites, I was able to process some of my thoughts and think how to carry out my research. I was able to ponder on different aspects of my research and decide whether or not to do certain things. This artifact in particular is one freewrite done at the beginning of the semester to start thinking about and noticing different genres in our lives. This helped get a feel for what genres were as I had some confusion at the beginning of the semester.
